About Me

My name is Tony Nolan and I am new to coding. I like to race RC cars and fly drones in my spare time. I worked in live events for over 18 years until COVID kicked in and now I am here. I went to culinary school 25 years ago and still enjoy cooking at home. I ran my on kitchen in a hotel for about 6 years before going in live events for hotels. I was a tech for PSAV for about 8 years before moving up to lead a crew. I ran my department for 10 years and help open a brand new hotel in the Aurora area. I look forward to learn and grow with the team.
